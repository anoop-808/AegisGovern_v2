# Incident Report – Attack 2: Dormant Account Abuse

## Incident Summary

A former employee account (John Former) remained in the tenant and represented a dormant account risk.

The account could potentially be abused if credentials were compromised.

## Affected User

- User: John Former

## Evidence

- user_inventory.png
- john_former_login.png
- account_disabled.png
- access_denied_attack2.png

## Impact

Dormant accounts increase the attack surface and may allow unauthorized access.

## Detection

The account was identified during identity review activities.

## Response

- Account disabled.
- Login attempts blocked.

## Lessons Learned

Dormant and former employee accounts should be reviewed regularly and disabled when no longer required.

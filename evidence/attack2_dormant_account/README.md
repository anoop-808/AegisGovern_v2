# Attack 2: Dormant Employee Account Abuse

## Objective

Simulate a former employee account that remains active after termination.

## Attack Simulation

The account John Former represented a user who had left the organization. The account remained available and was used to demonstrate the risks associated with inactive account management.

## Detection

The investigation reviewed:

* User Inventory
* Account Status
* Authentication Access
* Administrative Controls

## Evidence

* john_former_login.png
* account_disabled.png
* access_denied_attack2.png
* user_inventory.png

## Remediation

* Disable accounts immediately after employee departure
* Periodically review inactive accounts
* Implement account lifecycle management
